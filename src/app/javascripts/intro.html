Welcome to HTML5 Camera!

This site is an experiment with HTML5 technologies, including WebRTC, Device Orientation, Canvas, Video and others.

In order to use this site, you will need a browser that is on the bleeding edge of HTML5. Currently, HTML5 Camera Supports the following browsers...

  • Chrome Dev Channel
  • Chrome Canary
  • Opera Labs Camera Build

For the Chrome Builds, you will need to turn on the experimental WebRTC feature by entering chrome://flags in your address bar and then enabling Media Streaming
